## Meta Requirements

### Literature Review Alignment Checklist

Literature reviews synthesize and organize existing knowledge. Your writing should:

- [ ] **Systematic methodology** — Clear search strategy, inclusion/exclusion criteria
- [ ] **Comprehensive coverage** — Major works included, gaps acknowledged
- [ ] **Critical analysis** — Not just summary, but evaluation and synthesis
- [ ] **Taxonomy clarity** — Organize works into meaningful categories
- [ ] **Gap identification** — What's missing, underexplored, or contradictory
- [ ] **Future directions** — Where the field should go

**Literature Review Voice Examples:**
- ✅ "We identified 87 papers through systematic search. After screening, 34 met our inclusion criteria..."
- ✅ "Three distinct approaches emerge: [A], [B], and [C], differing primarily in..."
- ✅ "A notable gap exists in [area]. Only 3 of 34 papers address [X]..."
- ❌ "Many papers have been written about [X]" (vague, unquantified)
- ❌ "Paper A says X, Paper B says Y" (listing without synthesis)
- ❌ "This paper is the best" (subjective without criteria)

## Writing Notes

### Literature Review Guidelines

| Do | Don't |
|----|-------|
| Document methodology | Do ad-hoc searching |
| Categorize meaningfully | Just list papers |
| Synthesize across works | Summarize each separately |
| Identify gaps explicitly | Only describe what exists |
| Be quantitative about coverage | Make vague claims |
| Acknowledge limitations | Claim completeness |

### Synthesis vs. Summary

- **Summary:** Paper A did X. Paper B did Y.
- **Synthesis:** Two approaches exist: X (Papers A, C, E) and Y (Papers B, D). X excels at [Z], while Y offers [W].

The review's value is in synthesis, not summary.

### Managing Scope

- Define scope clearly and early
- Explain exclusions
- Don't apologize for not covering everything
- "Out of scope" is fine if justified

### Zotero Workflow

1. Import all candidates during search
2. Tag with inclusion/exclusion decisions
3. Add notes for synthesis
4. Export bibliography at end
5. Verify citation formatting
