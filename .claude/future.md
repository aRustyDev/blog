## Themes

- Minimal/Clean: Lots of whitespace, simple typography, distraction-free reading
- Technical/Hacker: Terminal-inspired, monospace fonts, dark themes, code-forward
- Modern/Polished: Smooth animations, gradients, contemporary design trends
- Documentation-like: Structured navigation, sidebar TOC, reference-style layout

## Features

- **Reading experience**: Typography, readability, content focus
- **Discoverability**: Search, tags, categories, related posts
- **Code presentation**: Syntax highlighting, copy buttons, code blocks
- **Portfolio hybrid**: Projects showcase alongside blog posts

## Colors

- System-adaptive: Follow OS preference, both modes equal
