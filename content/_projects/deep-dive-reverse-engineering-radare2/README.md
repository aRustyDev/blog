## Overview

A deep-dive on Radare2 (and Rizin/Cutter) covering the command-line-first philosophy, the composable command system, r2 utilities (rabin2, rahash2, ragg2), visual mode, r2pipe scripting, the Rizin fork and Cutter GUI, debugging, and binary patching. CLI-first approach: r2 is the vim of reverse engineering — powerful, cryptic, and deeply rewarding once learned.
