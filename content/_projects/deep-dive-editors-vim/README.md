## Overview

A deep-dive on Vim covering its modal editing philosophy (normal/insert/visual/command modes), the grammar of editing (verb + modifier + noun), registers and macros, the buffer/window/tab model, Vimscript and Vim 9 script, the plugin ecosystem, and why Vim knowledge transfers to every Vim-like tool. Grammar-first approach: Vim's composable editing language is its enduring contribution to computing.
